# AeroScroll

**Stop Wasting Time. Take Back Your Life.**

AeroScroll is a tool that shows you how much time you spend on Instagram Reels. It does more than just track your screen time. It helps you be more mindful of how you use your time.

I was stuck in a cycle of scrolling and I'm sure you have been too. You open Instagram for a minute. Before you know it 45 minutes are gone. This kind of content is designed to be addictive keeping you hooked while your real goals get ignored.

**That's why I created AeroScroll.**

It shows you how reels you've scrolled through and gives you a countdown to start fresh. It's not about restricting you. About making you aware of how you spend your time.

## How It Works

AeroScroll uses Androids **Accessibility Service** to detect when you interact with Instagram Reels.

1. **It Tracks Your Scrolls**: The app detects when you scroll through reels on Instagram.

2. **Counts Each Reel**: It uses logic to count each reel only once.

3. **Live Dashboard**: You can see your reel count on a simple dashboard with a live countdown timer.

4. **Compete with Friends**: See who can scroll the least on the leaderboard. The goal is to be at the bottom. That means you're living life, not just scrolling!

## Tech Stack

AeroScroll is built with Android tech:

* **UI Framework**: [Jetpack Compose](https://developer.android.com/jetpack/compose) for an engaging interface.

* **Architecture**: MVVM with Clean Architecture principles.

* **Database**: [Room Database](https://developer.android.com/training/data-storage/room) for local storage.

* **Styling**: Custom Material 3 theme with gradients and dark-mode optimization.

* **Testing**: Robolectric and Roborazzi for testing.
